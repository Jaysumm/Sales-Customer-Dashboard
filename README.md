# Sales & Customer Dashboard using Tableau

## Project Overview

Built an interactive Tableau dashboard to monitor sales performance,
customer behavior, profitability, and yearly growth.

---

## Business Problem

Business managers need an interactive dashboard to answer:

• How are sales changing?

• Which products are profitable?

• Which customers contribute the most?

• Which months perform best?

• How is customer acquisition changing?

## Key Insights

## Sales Performance
1. Sales grew by 15.0% compared to the previous year, indicating strong overall business growth in 2023.
2. Profit increased by 30.3% year-over-year, significantly outpacing sales growth. This suggests improved operational efficiency, better pricing strategies, or a more profitable product mix.
3. Product quantity sold increased by 26.8%, showing increased customer demand and higher order volumes.
4. November recorded the highest sales performance, while February was the weakest month, highlighting clear seasonal trends that can be used for inventory and marketing planning.
5. Several sub-categories generated high sales but low or negative profits, indicating opportunities to review pricing, discounts, or procurement costs for underperforming products.
6. Sales and profit remained above the yearly average for most of the second half of the year, suggesting stronger business performance during the later months.
## Customer Performance
1. The customer base increased by 8.6% compared to the previous year, reflecting successful customer acquisition and retention efforts.
2. Sales per customer increased by 5.9%, indicating that customers, on average, contributed more revenue than in the previous year.
3. Total customer orders grew by 28.3%, showing a significant increase in purchasing activity and customer engagement.
4. Most customers placed only one or two orders, while only a small percentage became repeat buyers. This presents an opportunity to improve customer retention through loyalty programs and personalized marketing.
5. A small group of customers contributed a disproportionately high share of total profit, emphasizing the importance of retaining high-value customers through targeted relationship management.
